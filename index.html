Acute Space is a curated gallery and performance venue focusing on the value of in-person creative experience and exchange between artist and patron. Acute aims for this experience to be as equitable in resources as possible to all involved, and remove as many points of dividing resources from the result of an event as we are able.

Mission

Acute Space aims to present a variety of artforms and events in an environment where everyone feels and is welcome.

What we do

We host music shows, poetry readings, dance, performance art, and workshops. We also present solo artist exhibitions in the main room and video art exhibitions in the red room along with visiting artists and pop-ups. We will have local artist merchandise for sale in addition to curated selections of music, books, and art.
